Giftmate Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Giftmate helps you manage wishlists, occasions, gift ideas, follows, and group gifting. To provide the service, we store profile information such as your username, display name, birthday, interests, avatar, follows, wishlists, gift activity, and app usage events.

We use Supabase to authenticate users and store app data. We also process Giftmate AI requests through our server so we can generate gift ideas for signed-in users.

We use analytics events to understand product usage, improve features, and diagnose errors. We do not sell your personal data.

You can request account deletion at any time from inside the app or by emailing support@giftm8.app. Deletion requests are processed manually unless a self-serve deletion tool is explicitly offered inside the product.

If you have any privacy questions, contact support@giftm8.app.
